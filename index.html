Research

My research studies a family of elliptic curves of the form y² = x³ − m²x + m² over the field of rational numbers.

The main focus of this work is to investigate rational points and rank-related properties of these curves. Using number-theoretic arguments, I study double points for certain subfamilies and prove the linear independence of explicitly constructed infinite-order points in Eₘ(Q) under suitable congruence conditions on m.
